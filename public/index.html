Cab, Tempo Traveller & Bus Rental Services — Comfortable Travel Made Easy

SwiftRide Travel offers trusted cab, tempo traveller, and bus rental services across India. Whether you are planning a family trip, corporate outing, pilgrimage, wedding, or airport transfer — we have the right vehicle for every journey.

Choose from a wide fleet including sedans, SUVs, 9-seater, 12-seater, 16-seater and 20-seater Tempo Travellers, mini buses and full-size Volvo buses. All vehicles are well-maintained, air-conditioned and driven by experienced, verified drivers to ensure a safe and comfortable ride every time.

🚗 Starting from just Rs.12/km for cabs and Rs.22/km for Tempo Travellers. We follow a completely transparent pricing policy — no hidden charges. Book online in minutes and get instant confirmation.
